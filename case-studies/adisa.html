I rebuilt a 30-year firm's entire web presence

A client engagement: I migrated Adisa Communications — a strategic communications firm with three decades of legacy — from a slow, vulnerable WordPress site to a hardened, instant static web app on Cloudflare's edge. Solo, end to end.

01The brief

My client's website carried 30 years of legacy — and 30 years of technical debt. It ran on a traditional database-driven WordPress stack: prone to security vulnerabilities, slow page reloads, and plugin bloat. They needed to look like a firm that handles serious contracts; the aging site quietly undercut that on every visit.

My mandate wasn't a visual refresh. It was to rebuild the foundation underneath — preserving three decades of their content and SEO equity while replacing the engine entirely.

02How I approached it — "scrape & rebuild"

  • Deep content extraction. I algorithmically scraped the existing site, preserving 30 years of photos and articles — and kept the original link structure so none of their SEO value was lost.
  • Structured data migration. I converted all their posts and insights from a slow database into a clean, immutable data structure — so the new site performs zero database lookups and loads instantly.
  • Component architecture. I rebuilt the entire interface in React 19 as modular, reusable components — navigation to footer.

03What I engineered

🛡️ Hardened security

By removing the database and PHP layer I eliminated ~99% of common web vulnerabilities (SQL injection, XSS, DDoS saturation). I deploy it as a read-only, immutable artifact over strict HTTPS/TLS — there's nothing to deface.

🚀 Performance

I built it as a single-page app with aggressive asset preloading and intelligent caching, so critical images and a persistent background video are ready before the user sees the page.

✨ Premium UX

I used Framer Motion for fluid, scroll-triggered reveals, and engineered a global background video that plays continuously across navigation — an effect traditional sites can't achieve.

🔍 SEO & accessibility

Semantic HTML5, Open Graph rich-preview cards, and accessible labels/alt text throughout — built into the structure, not bolted on after.

04The outcome

The brief wasn't a new coat of paint — it was a new foundation. So I rebuilt the foundation.
  • Maintenance-free. No plugins to update, no database to crash — it runs the same in five years as it does today.
  • Global reliability. Replicated across Cloudflare's edge — instant whether a visitor loads from Austin or Tokyo.
  • Hardened. The whole class of database/server attacks simply doesn't apply anymore.
  • Near-zero cost. Static edge hosting drops infrastructure cost toward zero while scaling to handle massive traffic.
